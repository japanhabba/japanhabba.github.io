Individual Patronage

Show your personal support for Japan Habba. Choose a tier below to have your name inscribed on our Sumo Banzuke ranking board or on a decorative Chōchin lantern displayed at the venue.

What is a Banzuke Board?

A Banzuke board is a traditional sumo ranking list, written in bold sumo calligraphy on a large wooden board. Your name will be displayed on a custom Banzuke board at the venue, mirroring the authentic style of sumo rankings — a unique way to have your patronage permanently inscribed.

What is a Chōchin?

A Chōchin is a traditional Japanese paper lantern, often seen at festivals and shrine celebrations. Your name will be beautifully inscribed on a decorative Chōchin lantern displayed prominently at the venue — a glowing tribute to your contribution.
