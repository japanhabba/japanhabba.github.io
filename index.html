Bengaluru • January 9th & 10th, 2027

Japan Habba 2027

India's biggest celebration of Japanese culture returns for its landmark 21st Edition. Experience a legacy event bridging deep-rooted traditions and modern pop culture.

Building on our sold-out 2025 edition (15,000+ confirmed attendees, 110+ exhibitors, and 3M+ social reach), Japan Habba 2027 expands to the massive Palace Grounds, Chamara Vajra, expecting 25,000+ attendees over a weekend of absolute cultural immersion.

Experience the Magic

Step into the electric atmosphere of Japan Habba. Catch a glimpse of what awaits you at our upcoming edition!

✨ Key Attractions for JH27

  • ✓

    Habba Live (Premium Concert)

    India's first arena-style seated concert featuring 8+ international acts (1-day ticket: ₹1.5k~₹3k, 2-day VIP: ₹7.5k).

  • ✓

    The Versus Festival (Gaming)

    India's first international fighting game community major tournament. Compete or watch the hype matches live.

  • ✓

    75th Anniversary Museum

    A centerpiece venue-wide art installation commemorating the 75th Anniversary of India-Japan diplomatic relations.

  • ✓

    Anime Pavilion & Specials

    Japanese Voice Actor panels, panels by studios traveling from Japan, and sword dance performances by "Shidenryu".

JH27 Commemorative Theme

India-Japan Relations

Japan Habba 2027 is an official commemoration of the 75th anniversary of India-Japan diplomatic relations.

📜

Historical Connections

  • • Honors legacy of Subhas Chandra Bose & Rash Behari Bose.
  • • Highlights historic contributions of Radhabinod Pal ("Judge Pal").
🚇

Contemporary Collaborations

  • • Showcasing infrastructure funded and jointly sponsored by JICA.
  • • Special focus on Bangalore's urban metro projects.
🖌️

Anime & Manga Crossovers

  • • Spotlighting Japanese works featuring India as a key location.
  • • Celebrating the growth of creations by Indian artists.
🎮

Video Game Crossovers

  • • Celebrating Dhalsim (the classic Indian Street Fighter fighter).
  • • Featuring Arjun (Street Fighter 6 new Indian character).
